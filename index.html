Details of in-resort taxes

From 1st of July 2016, the Balearic Govt. are charging a sustainable tourism tax on all overnight stays which will be payable on arrival in the hotel:

  • 5-star & 4-star superior hotels: €2 per night
  • 4-star & 3-star superior hotel: €1.5 per night
  • 1-star, 2-star & 3-star hotels: €1 per night
  • 4-key & 4-key superior tourist apartments: €2 per night
Exclusions: Children under the age of 16 are exempt from the tax, 50% discount during the off-peak season (Nov 1st - Apr 30th) & 50% discount from the tenth night of any stay onwards
